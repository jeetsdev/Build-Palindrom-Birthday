Is your Birth-Date is a Palindrome number.

A palindromic number is a number that remains the same when its digits are reversed. Example - 2012/12/02

This app checks your birthdate in 4 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yy, m-dd-yyyy. Example - if your birthdate is 01 Aug 1995, then app will check for 19950801, 01081995, 080195,1081995.
